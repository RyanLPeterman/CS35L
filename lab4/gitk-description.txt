Upper Right Window: Shows the timestamp for the commit
Upper Middle Window: Shows the author of the commit along with email
Upper Left Window: Shows the layout of the list of commits
	with additional information about merges and branches
	
Bottom Left Window: Shows a diff of the changes to the files
Middle Left Window: Shows all information about a commit
Bottom Right Window: Shows the files that were impacted by the commit

Thus this allows us to visualize graphically what is inside of the git
data structures in terms of the flow of the commits as well as the actual
internals of the commits and their effects.
